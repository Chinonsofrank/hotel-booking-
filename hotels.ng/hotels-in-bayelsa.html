About Bayelsa

Bayelsa is an oil-rich State located in the heart of southern Nigeria. An estimated 2 million people reside in the State. Many of the communities in Bayelsa are surrounded by water and are therefore inaccessible by road. Its Capital city, Yenagoa is home to the larger block of its people who are predominantly Ijaw.
