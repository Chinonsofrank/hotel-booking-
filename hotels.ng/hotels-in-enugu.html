About Enugu

Enugu is a southeastern Nigerian State that was formed in 1991 after being carved out from the old Anambra State. It is home to an estimated 3.5 million people who are predominantly Igbo. Its Capital city Enugu and it is arguably one of the most remarkable cities east of the River Niger.
