About Oyo

Oyo state is home to some of the most beautiful Yoruba people, with Ibadan as its capital city. This ancient and beautiful city exudes the natural hospitality that the people are renowned for. The state is home to the prestigious University of Ibadan and other iconic landmarks. An estimated 6.5 million people live in Oyo State.

Luxury hotels in Oyo state tend to cluster around the high-brow regions, where people get around via exotic vehicles and other forms of public transportation. You can book from a range of luxury hotels and affordable hotels across the State using Hotels.ng and benefit from the excellent service available.
