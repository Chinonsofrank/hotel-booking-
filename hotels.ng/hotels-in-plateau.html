About Plateau

Plateau State is a central Nigerian State famous for its beauty and unique ambience. It is fondly and aptly named the Home of Peace and Tourism. The State capital is Jos. An estimated 3.2 million people live in Plateau State and there is a lot of travel activity recorded yearly due to its tourism attractions.
